TenantSage™

Governed Knowledge Architecture

TenantSage™ is not a chatbot, AI model, or RAG tool. It is an architectural framework that governs how knowledge is stored, accessed, retrieved, and safely exposed to AI systems.

Core Architectural Pillars

Family-Tenant Model

Each organisation operates as a hard-isolated tenant. Branches remain strictly separated unless explicitly authorised.

Embedded Governance

Role-based access, assignment scope, and data validity are enforced at the database and retrieval layers.

Controlled Retrieval

AI systems receive only approved, in-scope documents. Unauthorised data is never visible to the model.

Designed For

  • Hospitality and property groups
  • Healthcare and education networks
  • Government and regulated environments
  • Enterprise multi-site operators
